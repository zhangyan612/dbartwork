ABOUT ME

Ever since I was a little girl, I've always loved to fill up my school notebooks with sketches and picture scenarios. I always sat around watching too many cartoons and reading children’s books and comics over and over. The looming result was spending the next 22 years creating entertaining cartoons and short comic strips.

Drawing is broadly not only a way to express myself and to tell a story, but each drawing is like an inside joke I want to share and have it relate with each and every one of you. Becoming an illustrator has always been my preferred major to work for, but I have an interest and skill for other medias as well, such as character design, surface design, sequential art, painting, animation, and graphic design. I also enjoy collaborating with brands & designers that compliment my personal style, receiving exciting opportunities to work with some of the best brands out there that allows me to try new things and also apply to my creative side as well!
